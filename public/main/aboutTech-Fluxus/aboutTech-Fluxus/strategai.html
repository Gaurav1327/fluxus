StrategAI

StrategAI is an AI-based contest where the players are pitted against each other in an interesting Strategy game.
Being our first time, we are taking MIT’s “BATTLECODE” event as our inspiration.
Students from anywhere in India can register for this event as a team of at most 4. Students will be given 1½ months of time for submission of the final code. During this time, the teams are supposed to refer to the documentation, understand it, plan strategies and implement their AI on our game. The code of the game will be released during start of December.
Based on the number of teams registered, we are planning to make the matching system either a pool based or a knockout tournament. Each match has 3 rounds. The team which wins 2 rounds first wins.
