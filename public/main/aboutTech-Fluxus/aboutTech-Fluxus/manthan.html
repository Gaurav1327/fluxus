“Climate change is no longer some far-off problem, it is happening here, it is happening now.” -Barack Obama
Climate change is a global issue and is posing one of the biggest long-term threats to society. Manthan, a hackathon competition aims at resolving issues related to climate change. It consists of 2 problem statements dealing with flood disaster management and safe green housing. Participants are expected to come up with creative technological solutions to the following day to day problems by the use of both innovation and technology
