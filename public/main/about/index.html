WHAt IS FLUXUS?

Fluxus, IIT Indore's annual techno-cultural fest is the Central India's biggest and most anticipated event. With shows from renowned artists like Farhan Akhtar, Sunidhi Chauhan, Shirley Setia and Amit Trivedi to up-and-coming performers like The Local Train, Fluxus has witnessed exhilarating performances from both Indian and international artists alike. Our annual Fashion Flagship Event, 'Campus Princess', adorned Miss India Koyal Rana as a judge in the year 2019. Entrepreneurs and leaders such as Farrhad Acidwalla have addressed the students and visitors at Fluxus.

With preparations to exceed the 25,000 footfall of Fluxus '19 ongoing, expectations are that Fluxus '20 will be its biggest edition yet.
