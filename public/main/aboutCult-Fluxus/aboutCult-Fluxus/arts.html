Doodle Up

Team Size: Solo
Rounds: 2 rounds
Judging Criteria: Creativity, Theme incorporation, Relevance to theme, Complexity, Neatness, Overall Impact
Theme: On spot
Time Limit: 2 hours
Materials Provided: Pencil, eraser, a black gel pen, A4 size sheet and a rough A4 sized paper(material will be provided according to a chits based system).

Prizes

Coming soon

Schedule

Coming soon

Contact

Coming soon

Rules :


● The participants will have to doodle on the basis of a theme which will be given on the spot.
● Only the material provided by organizers is allowed to be used.
● No references are allowed to be carried by the participants. Any explicit content shall lead to straight disqualification.
● Only one sheet would be given. So, the participants should take special care so as to not spoil the sheet. An A4 sized paper would be given which could be used for rough work.
● Participants are advised to come 0.5 hour prior to competition.
● The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
● The participants are required to bring their own newspapers.
● In case of any on the spot discrepancy, the decision of the organizers will be considered final.

Live Sketching

Team Size :Solo Judging criteria (100 marks): Proportion & Perspective: 35
Composition and Components: 35
Shading: 30

Prizes

Coming soon

Schedule

Coming soon

Contact

Coming soon

Rules :


● Medium: Graphite pencils / Shading pencils(will be provided if necessary). Charcoal and other colour pencils are not allowed.
● Drawing Sheet:- Off-White A3 sheet (Cartridge sheet quality) will be provided.
● Participants may bring their own drawing items like pencils, erasers, tissue paper, cotton and sharpener.
● Use of scale is not allowed in live sketching
● Drawing board, thick cardboard will be provided, that can serve as a base while drawing.
● Participants can bring some kind of base(for support, for example an A3 sized sketchbook, cardboard, etc.) on which they can keep their sheets.
● No external help (eg. internet/photograph on phone or camera) is allowed during the event.
