Contact

Rules :


RULES
● Participants will be given a time slot of 5 minutes in which he/she can perform any number of songs in Hindi or English or any regional language.
● There will be a penalty of 20% of points per 10 seconds after exceeding the time limit. After 30 seconds, the music will be stopped.
● In the case of regional songs, the participant needs to submit the lyrics in well written/printed format to the judges prior to the performance.
● The song can be a movie song, album song or an original composition.
● Participants can perform using a karaoke track or with one instrumental accompaniment.
● In the case of a backing track, the participant needs to bring the audio file either in a pen-drive or in his/her phone.
● The participantcan play own instrument(s). Else one accompaniment will be allowed where participantswon’t be allowed to play any instrument.
● Judging will be purely based on vocals.
● The decision of judges and coordinators shall be final in all cases.
