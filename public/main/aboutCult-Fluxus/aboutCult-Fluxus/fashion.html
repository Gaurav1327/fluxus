Description

Fashion Designing

Ever felt so thunder seeing someone so immaculately dressed that you run out of cheers and words. Fashion Hunt , where the most glamorous people show off their skills as they walk the ramp, a fashion show evoking all those creative peeps to put on their thinking caps and come out with the most innovative theme-based fashion show.

Prizes

Coming soon

Schedule

Coming soon

Contact

Coming soon

Rules :


RULES
● Every team has to mail a design after registration on contactus@fluxus.co.in after registering on website.
● Minimum number of models per team is 4.
● Minimum age limit is 16 years.
● Teams can take help from professional designers, choreographers and makeup artists. Only 1 choreographer, make-up artist and maximum of 4 designers will be accepted from one team.
● These professionals will be considered as a part of team and will be eligible for the Best Choreographer, the Best Makeup Artist and the Best Designer Award.
● Choreographers, makeup artists and designers can also be ramp models.
● On exceeding the time limit, negative points will be awarded.
● The teams are responsible for their own music and sequence.
● Music is to be submitted in a format compatible with Windows media player (preferably in mp3 format). Music is to be submitted beforehand by sending it to contactus@fluxus.co.in
● If lighting facility is to be availed, one person of the group can supervise the lighting. Otherwise it will be up to the lights group
● Water, Fire and animals in any form will not be allowed on the stage or on the ramp.
● Vulgarity in any form will not be allowed in the competition. If the team feels any stunt could be termed as vulgar, it should speak to the organiser before performing it on the stage.
● Organisers reserve the right to cancel any round and disqualify teams who do not adhere to the rules
● The decision of the organisers will be the final one.
● Only selected teams will move to the final round.
● Rules might undergo slight changes depending on the situation.
Special Rewards will be given for best model, best choreographer, best makeup and best designer.
PRELIMS THEME
BOHEMIAN STYLE
In modern use, the term "Bohemian" is applied to people who live unconventional, usually artistic, lives. Use your creativity and imagination to make the best out of this theme. Not following the theme immediately disqualifies the team.
FINALS THEME
This round is theme-less. That is to say, teams can decide their own themes that they will not declare beforehand. After their performance, they can declare it, and then judges may decide the quality of the performance. Alternatively, the teams can go without a theme and say so after they have performed.
JUDGING CRITERIA
Collection, design and finish:
Creativity / Innovation in design - 20%
Standard of Design - 10%
Originality - 10%
Uniqueness - 20%
Product quality and finish - 10%
Concept or Vision - 30%

To be welcomed to the tribe you, too, must impress the Headman with your... eh, skills, for lack of a better word. Hilarity ensues when one is asked to charm the ladies and win over the men over the competition, but you’ve got to do what you’ve got to do! Bottomline : Be the stand-out person at Fluxus’20

Voice of Fluxus

Shows like 'Sa Re Ga Ma Pa' have introduced you to the glamour of performing on stage and vowing a ​diverse audience with your voice. 'Indian Idol' and 'The Voice' have kept that going. Talent, long hours of practice and the charisma to dazzle- you need them all to hold your own this year. Experience heightened heartbeats and melodious music, fight and sing it out as you contest to become the Voice of this year’s FLUXUS.

Prizes

Coming soon

Schedule

Coming soon

Contact

Coming soon

Rules :


RULES
● Participants will be given a time slot of 5 minutes in which he/she can perform any number of songs in Hindi or English or any regional language.
● There will be a penalty of 20% of points per 10 seconds after exceeding the time limit. After 30 seconds, the music will be stopped.
● In the case of regional songs, the participant needs to submit the lyrics in well written/printed format to the judges prior to the performance.
● The song can be a movie song, album song or an original composition.
● Participants can perform using a karaoke track or with one instrumental accompaniment.
● In the case of a backing track, the participant needs to bring the audio file either in a pen-drive or in his/her phone.
● The participantcan play own instrument(s). Else one accompaniment will be allowed where participantswon’t be allowed to play any instrument.
● Judging will be purely based on vocals.
● The decision of judges and coordinators shall be final in all cases.
